Graduate Class Final Project

Age Friendly NYC

  • Location: New York City
  • Client: AIA, NYC
  • Project date: September 2020 - January 2021
  • Project URL: Snoopeep.GitHub.io/EqMap

This project was completed for my Big Data class, in collaboration with two classmates. The New York chapter of the American Institute of Architects was our client, looking for big data based problem identification for the biggest issues facing New York today.

My team and I use big data, Python, ArcGIS, and HTML coding to create interactive maps showing the problem of inequitable amenity access for the senior residents of New York. My personal contribution was primarily the data collection and analysis in GIS.
