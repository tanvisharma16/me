Architecture

Cira Tower II

  • Location: Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • Client: Brandywine Realty Trust
  • Project date: September 2013 - June 2014
  • Project URL: FMCTower.com

During my 1 year preceptorship with Pelli Clarke Pelli Architects, I spent the majority of my time designing the public spaces and outer shell of this mixed use tower in Philadelphia. The lower half of this tower hosts office spaces, while the upper half serves as residences, primarily targeting University of Pennsylvania affiliates. I worked on curtain wall design, ground flood interior planning, including a dynamic ceiling design, and exterior site plan, including circulation and a covered drop off zone. As a member of a three person team, I also played a large role in putting together a Design Development set in Revit.
