Architecture

Riverside Center One West End

  • Location: New York, New York
  • Client: Silverstein Properties
  • Project date: September 2013 - June 2014

During the course of a 1 year preceptorship with Pelli Clarke Pelli Architects, I worked on detail designs for the curtainwall of the luxury tower as well as facade details for the affordable housing portion of this project. This project taught me a lot about the strictly formulaic designing of residential towers in Manhattan, due to the economic necessity of maximizing rentable space.
